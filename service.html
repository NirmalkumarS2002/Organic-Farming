help FAQ

Questions We Hear Often

Can't find your answer? Reach out to us on WhatsApp — we reply within the hour.

How do I know the produce is truly organic?

add

Every product carries a QR code that traces its origin to a specific field and farmer. Our farm holds NPOP, USDA Organic, and EU Organic certifications — all verified by independent third-party auditors annually.

What areas do you deliver to?

add

We currently deliver across Karur, Trichy, and select areas of Coimbatore. Same-day delivery is available within Karur for orders placed before 9 AM. We are expanding to Chennai by late 2025.

Can I cancel or pause my subscription?

add

Yes, absolutely. You can pause or cancel your weekly subscription at any time with no penalty. Just message us on WhatsApp before Wednesday midnight and your next basket will be skipped or your plan cancelled.

Do you offer farm tours for school groups?

add

Yes! We offer special group rates for schools and colleges. Children under 10 visit for free. We conduct interactive sessions on seed sowing, composting, and harvest. Book at least 7 days in advance for group visits.

What if my delivery arrives damaged or late?

add

We have a no-questions-asked freshness guarantee. If your produce arrives damaged, wilted, or more than 2 hours late, we will replace it at no charge or issue a full refund — whichever you prefer. Just send us a photo on WhatsApp.
