history_edu Our Story

From a Small Plot of Land to a Thriving Organic Farm

It all began in 2009 when our founder, Arjun Murugan, returned to his hometown in Karur with one simple idea — to grow food the way his grandparents did, free from chemicals and full of nutrition.

What started as a small family plot has grown into over 200 acres of certified organic farmland, serving thousands of families across Tamil Nadu with fresh, traceable produce.

"The soil is not just dirt. It is a living ecosystem that feeds us. When we protect it, it protects us."
— Arjun Murugan, Founder

Today, our team of 80+ farmers practices traditional intercropping, rainwater harvesting, and natural composting — ensuring that every vegetable, fruit, and grain we grow carries the best of the earth.
